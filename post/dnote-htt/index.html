terms of $\prod (a:A), P(a)$ are sections of $P$ := functions from term of $A$ to term of type $P(a)$.

each judgement defines a term:
$a:A \vdash g(a):P(a)$ defines $\lambda a.g(a):\prod(a:A),P(a)$

$g(a):P(a)$, $x:A$, ($\beta$-rule) $evaluate(\lambda a.g(a),x)=g(x)$
$f:\prod(a:A),P(a)$, $P(a)$ be a section of $P$, ($\eta$-rule) $\lambda a.evaluate(f,a) = f$
