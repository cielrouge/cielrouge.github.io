corollary 1.4.17
这样的skew-field是交换的：代数闭域上的有限维向量空间.
若$A$是有限维半简单$K$-代数，且$K$是代数闭域，则存在$n_i$ s.t.
$$A\simeq \prod_{j=1}^m Mat_{n_j\times n_j} (K)$$
proof.
使$D$是$K$上的有限维skew-field，$d\in D$.
因为$D$是有限维$K$-向量空间，由cayley-hamilton定理，d有最小多项式$P_d(X)\in K[X]$

corollary 1.4.20
若$A$是半简单有限维$K$-代数，$S$是简单$A$-模，则$\exists i$ s.t.
$$ S\simeq S_i \simeq \begin{pmatrix} D_i \\ \vdots\\ D_i\end{pmatrix}_{n_i} $$
proof.
take $s\in S\backslash \lbrace 0 \rbrace$, $A\cdot s$是$S$非零子模， $\because$ $S$ simple, $\therefore$ $S=As$, $\therefore$ $S$是$ _{A}A$商模.
$\because$ $ _{A}A$ semisimple, $\therefore$ $\exists i$ s.t. $S\simeq S_i$.
$$S_i \simeq Hom_A( _{A}A, S_i) \simeq Hom_A(\bigoplus_{l=1}^m \bigoplus_{j=1}^{n_i} S_l,S_i) \simeq \begin{pmatrix} D_i \\ \vdots \\D_i\end{pmatrix}_{n_i}$$

by remark 1.4.12, every simple A-module S is a vector space over the skew field $End_A(S)$.
