K-代数：
一个 环结构(A)
一个 交换环(K)到 环结构的中心 的环同态

代数同态:
环同态:A -> B, 结构同态:(K -> A) -> (K -> B)
$\forall \lambda \in K,\forall a \in A,\phi (\lambda \cdot a)=\lambda\cdot\phi (a)$)

(left A-module M:
an abelian group with a mapping $\mu: A\times M\to M$)

M是A-模 => 有K-代数同态 $\lambda :A\to End_K (M)$.
给定K-代数同态$\nu: A\to End_K(M)$ => M上有唯一A-模结构，使$A\to End_K(M)$是$\nu$.

对each $a\in A$, 定义$\underline{\lambda_a:M\to M}$, $\lambda_a:m\mapsto \mu (a,m) =: a\cdot m$. （由A是$K$-代数，$K\to Z(A)$，证得$\lambda$的K-线性.）
对$\nu: A\to End_K(M)$, 定义$\mu:A\times M\to M$, $\mu (a,m):=\underline{\nu (a)(m)}$


一个表示是, 通过一个RealWorldObject上的作用, 一个抽象群的具体实现.

群在域上的一个n阶表示: $G \to GL_n(K)$

用 相同形状的可逆矩阵的 线性组合 形成 方阵；
用 群元的 K-线性组合 形成 K-代数.
(=> 群环的概念)

$$RG := \bigoplus_{g\in G} R\cdot e_g \mid R \cdot e_g = R , \forall g \in G$$ $$(\sum_{h_1\in G} r_{h_1}e_{h_1})\cdot (\sum_{h_2\in G} s_{h_2}e_{h_2}) := \sum_{g\in G}(\sum_{h\in G} r_h s_{h^{-1} g})e_g$$ $$G\to (RG)^\times, g\mapsto 1\cdot e_g$$


群->代数的单位群 开拓到 群环->代数

$$\alpha : G \to A^{\times}, \beta : RG \to A, \alpha = \beta\mid_{G}$$ $$\beta(\sum_{g\in G} r_ge_g):=\sum_{g\in G}r_g\alpha (g)$$

$$G \to GL_n(R)\quad (= Mat_{n\times n}(R)^{\times})$$ $$RG \to End_R(R^n)\quad (=Mat_{n\times n}(R))$$ $$\phi: G\to GL_n(R)$$ $$\widehat{\phi}:RG\ni \sum_{g\in G} r_g e_g\mapsto \sum_{g\in G} r_g\phi (g)\in End_R(R^n)$$

任意$KG$-模$M$, 任意$M$子模$N$, 存在$M$子模$L$ s.t. $M\simeq N\oplus L$:
设$N$是$M$非零子模, $\tilde{L}:=M/N$,
$\pi:M\to\tilde{L},m\mapsto m+N$,
$K$-线性映射:$\tilde{\rho}:\tilde{L}\to M, l+N\mapsto l,\forall l\in \mathcal{L}$.
$\pi \circ \tilde{\rho}=id_{\tilde{L}}$, $\pi$是$KG$-模同态, $\tilde{\rho}$未必
定义: $\rho: \tilde{L}\to M, l+N\mapsto \frac{1}{\vert G\vert}\sum_{g\in G}e_g^{-1} \tilde{\rho}(e_g\cdot (l+N))$
$\because \vert G\vert$在$K$中可逆, $\therefore \frac{1}{\vert G\vert}\in K$
$\pi \circ \rho=id_{\tilde{L}}$
$\forall l+N\in \tilde{L}, \forall h\in G, \rho(e_h \cdot (l+N))=e_h\cdot \rho(l+N)$ =>
$\rho$是$KG$-模同态, $L:=im(\rho)$是$M$的$KG$-子模.

($R$是交换环, $A$是$R$-代数, $M$是$A$-模)

$(\rho \circ \pi) \circ (\rho \circ \pi) = \rho \circ (\pi \circ \rho) \circ \pi = \rho \circ id_{ \tilde{L} } \circ \pi = \rho \circ \pi$ (idempotent)
$\because \pi \circ \rho = id_{ \tilde{L} }\quad \therefore \rho$ 单射

$M$ is Noetherian: (ascending to stationary)
if $\exists M_1\subseteq M_2\subseteq … \subseteq M$ then $\exists n_0\in \mathbb{N}$ s.t. $M_n=M_{n_0}, \forall n \geq n_0$
$M$ is artinian: (desceding to stationary)
if $\exists M\supseteq M_1 \supseteq M_2 \supseteq …$ then $\exists n_0\in \mathbb{N}$ s.t. $M_n=M_{n_0}, \forall n \geq n_0$

则任意有限维$A$-模作为$K$-向量空间Noetherian且artinian.

等价命题：
1. $M$ is Noetherian
2. $M$的所有子模都是有限生成的
3. 对$M$的任意子模$N$, $N$与$M/N$ are Noetherian

1=>2:
设$N$是$M$的子模, $N$的生成集: $\lbrace m_i \vert i\in I\rbrace$ ($I$ is not finite)
对每一个$n\in N$, 找到$m(n)\in \lbrace m_i \vert i\in I\rbrace$ (inductively)
s.t. $m(n)\notin \sum_{j=1}^{n-1} A\cdot m(j)$
由此构造了$M$的$A$-子模: $M(n):=\sum_{j=1}^{n} A\cdot m(j)$
$M(n-1) \subsetneqq M(n), \forall n\in \mathbb{N}$,
与$M$ is Noetherian相悖.


等价命题:
1. $M$ is artinian
2. 对$M$的任意子模$N$, $N$与$M/N$ are artinian

$M$ Noetherian iff. 每个非空的 $M$的子模 的集合, 都有最大元.
$M$ artinian iff. 每个非空的 $M$的子模 的集合, 都有最小元.

$$ \varphi =\begin{pmatrix} \varphi_{U,X} & \varphi_{V,X} \\ \varphi_{U,Y} & \varphi_{V,Y} \end{pmatrix} $$

theorem 1.4.16 (Wedderburn)

A be a finite dimensional semisimple K-algebra.
$\exists D_i$: skew-fields with centres containing K. ($D_i$, $n_i$: completely determined by A)
$$A \simeq \prod_{i=1}^m Mat_{n_i\times n_i}(D_i)$$

Proof.
by lemma 1.4.14, $A \simeq (End_A( _{A}A))^{op}$,
$\because$ A semisimple, $\therefore$ $ _{A}A$ isomorphic to diret sum of simple modules,
$$ _{A}A \simeq \bigoplus_{i=1}^m \bigoplus_{j=1}^{n_i} S_i$$
$$A\simeq (End_A(\bigoplus _{i=1}^m \bigoplus _{j=1}^{n_i} S_i))^{op}$$
by lemma 1.4.13, $Hom_A (S_i, S_j) = 0$,
$$A\simeq \prod_{i=1}^m (End_A (\prod_{j=1}^{n_i} S_i))^{op}$$
for A-modules X,Y,V,W,
$$Hom_A(X\oplus Y, V\oplus W) \simeq \begin{pmatrix} Hom_A(X,V) & Hom_A(Y,V)\\ Hom_A(X,W) & Hom_A(Y,W) \end{pmatrix}$$
$$\therefore End_A(\bigoplus_{j=1}^{n_i} S_i)\simeq Mat_{n_i\times n_i}(End_A(S_i))$$
by Schur’s lemma, $End_A(S_i)=D_i’$ is skew-field containing K.
if D is skew-field centre containing K, then $D^{op}$ either. for algebras A and B, since A, B commute in direct product, $(A\times B)^{op} \simeq A^{op} \times B^{op}$.
$\forall B$ $(Mat_{n\times n} (B))^{op} \simeq Mat_{n\times n} (B^{op})$, $M\mapsto M^T$.
$D_i := D_i’^{op}$.
unicity is consequence of: skew-fields are endomorphism algebras of the simple modules,
krull-schmidt theorem implies: the unicity of decomposition of regular module into a sum of simple submodules.
$n_i$: simple modules(同构等价类) 作为direct factor of the regular representation的重数.
