($K$:交换环, $A$:$K$-代数 $M$:$A$-模)
$M$ is Noetherian:
$\exists M_1\subseteq M_2\subseteq … \subseteq M$ $\quad$ =>
$\exists n_0\in \mathbb{N}$ s.t. $M_n=M_{n_0}, \forall n \geq n_0$
$M$ is artinian:
$\exists M\supseteq M_1 \supseteq M_2 \supseteq …$ $\quad$ =>
$\exists n_0\in \mathbb{N}$ s.t. $M_n=M_{n_0}, \forall n \geq n_0$

若$A$是域$K$上代数, 则任意有限维$A$-模作为$K$-向量空间, is Noetherian and artinian.

($A$:环, $M$: $A$上左模)
等价命题： 1. $M$ is Noetherian
2. $M$的所有子模都是有限生成的
3. 对$M$的任意子模$N$, $N$与$M/N$ are Noetherian

1=>2:
设$N$是$M$的子模, $N$的生成集: $\lbrace m_i \vert i\in I\rbrace$ ($I$ is not finite)
对每一个$n\in N$, 找到$m(n)\in \lbrace m_i \vert i\in I\rbrace$ (inductively)
s.t. $m(n)\notin \sum_{j=1}^{n-1} A\cdot m(j)$
由此构造了$M$的$A$-子模: $M(n):=\sum_{j=1}^{n} A\cdot m(j)$
$M(n-1) \subsetneqq M(n), \forall n\in \mathbb{N}$,
与$M$ is Noetherian相悖.


等价命题:
1. $M$ is artinian
2. 对$M$的任意子模$N$, $N$与$M/N$ are artinian

($A$: 代数, $M$: $A$-模)
$M$ Noetherian iff.
每个非空的 $M$的子模 的集合, 都有最大元.
$M$ artinian iff.
每个非空的 $M$的子模 的集合, 都有最小元.

Noetherian模: 满的自同态是单的

$\phi ^n := \phi \circ \phi^{n-1}$
M的递增子模序列: $(ker(\phi ^n))_{n\in \mathbb{N}}$
Noetherian => becomes stationary
$n_0 : ker(\phi ^n)=ker(\phi ^{n_0}),\forall n\geq n_0$
$m: m\in ker(\phi)$
$\phi ^k$ surjective => $\exists x\in M$ s.t. $\phi ^{n_0}(x)=m$,
$0=\phi(m)=\phi^{n_0 +1}(x)$, $x\in ker(\phi^{n_0 +1})=ker(\phi^{n_0})$,
$m=\phi^{n_0}(x)=0$, $\phi$ injective.
