(Krull-Schimidt)
($K$是域，$A$是$K$-代数，$M$是Noetherian且artinian的$A$-模.)
若有不可分解的A-模：$M_1,M_2,…,M_m$ 与 $N_1,N_2,…,N_n$ s.t. $\bigoplus_{j=1}^m M_j = M = \bigoplus_{i=1}^n N_i$
则 $\exists \sigma \in \mathfrak{S}_n$, s.t. $M_j \simeq N_{\sigma(j)}$

(Fitting)
对每一个自同态，存在一个模的分解，使这个自同态限制在一部分上是幂零自同态，限制在在另一部份上是自同构.($M$是Noetherian且artinian的$A$-模.)
$\forall u$ (endomorphism of M), $\exists M\simeq N\oplus S$ s.t.
$u\vert N$ is nilpotent endomorphism of N.
$u\vert S$ is automorphism of S.
may take $N=ker(u^m), S=im(u^m)$ (for m large enough)

local: 环的不可逆元的集合是一个双边理想.

$M$是非零不可分$A$模 =>
$End_A(M)$是一个局部环.
$M$的每个自同态either幂零or双射.

(M -> N -> M)是同构, 且N不可分, 则(M -> N), (N -> M)都是同构.
$M,N$是两个非零$A$-模, $N$不可分, $u\in Hom_A (M,N)$, $v\in Hom_A(N,M)$,
$v\circ u\in Aut_A(M)$ => $u$, $v$ 是同构

$\varphi: U\oplus V\to X\oplus Y$
$$ \varphi =\begin{pmatrix} \varphi_{U,X} & \varphi_{V,X} \\ \varphi_{U,Y} & \varphi_{V,Y} \end{pmatrix} $$
若$\varphi_{U,X}$是同构，则
$$ \tau =\begin{pmatrix} id_U & -\varphi_{U,X}^{-1}\circ \varphi_{V,X} \\ 0 & id_V \end{pmatrix} \in Aut_A(U\oplus V)$$
$\varphi \circ \tau: V\to Y$也是同构.
