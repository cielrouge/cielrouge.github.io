$f(\mathbf{x_i}) = \mathbf{w}^{T} \mathbf{x_i} + b$
$\hat{\mathbf{w}} = (\mathbf{w}; b)$
$$ \mathbf{X} = \begin{pmatrix} x_{11} & x_{12} & \hdots & x_{1d} & 1 \
x_{21} & x_{22} & \hdots & x_{2d} & 1 \
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \
x_{m1} & x_{m2} & \hdots & x_{md} & 1 \end{pmatrix} $$
$\hat{\mathbf{w}}^* = \underset{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}{arg min} (\mathbf{y} - \mathbf{X} \hat{\mathbf{w}})^T (\mathbf{y} - \mathbf{X} \hat{\mathbf{w}}) $
$\hat{\mathbf{w}}^* = (\mathbf(X^T X))^{-1} \mathbf{X}^T\mathbf{y}$
$\hat{\mathbf{x_i}} = (\mathbf{x_i};1)$
